# Privacy Policy - NAOMA Evolutivo - DIAL

**Last Updated: February 3, 2026**

## Overview

NAOMA Evolutivo - DIAL ("the App") is a ChatGPT application that provides 
14 unified AI architectures for enhanced analysis and problem-solving.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Query Content:** The questions and problems you submit for analysis
- **Architecture Selections:** Which DIAL architectures you activate

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Email addresses or contact information
- Payment information
- Location data
- Device identifiers

## Data Usage

Your queries are processed in real-time to:
1. Activate the selected DIAL architectures
2. Generate enhanced analysis prompts
3. Return structured responses to ChatGPT

**We do NOT:**
- Store your queries permanently
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for training or marketing
- Track your usage across sessions

## Data Retention

- Queries are processed in memory only
- No persistent storage of user data
- Session data is cleared when the connection ends

## Third-Party Services

This App operates as an MCP server that connects to:
- **OpenAI/ChatGPT:** Your queries are processed through ChatGPT's interface

## Security

- All connections use HTTPS encryption
- SSTAP Security architecture validates all inputs
- No sensitive data is logged or stored

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Know what data is processed
- Request deletion of any stored data (note: we don't store data)
- Opt out by not using the App

## Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be noted with a new "Last Updated" date.
